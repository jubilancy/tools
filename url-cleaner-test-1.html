Input

Paste raw text, a mixed note, or a block of URLs. Use the cleanup switches below to keep only the parts you want.

Result

The cleaned output keeps one URL per line, which works well for audits, migrations, and quick exports into other tools.

What gets removed

Common tracking keys such as utm_*, fbclid, gclid, msclkid, ref, mc_cid, and mc_eid.

Typical use

Turn noisy analytics URLs into canonical links before deduping, sharing, or importing elsewhere.
