AR Marker Viewer

point your camera at a hiro or kanji marker and see a 3d object appear — no app needed.

1.Print or display one of the markers below on your screen
2.Choose a 3D shape and color to overlay on the marker
3.Hit launch AR and allow camera access
4.Point your camera at the marker — the object will appear on top
AR Markers — print or display these
Hiro
the classic AR.js marker — most reliable
Kanji
alternative built-in AR.js marker

tip: display the marker image fullscreen on a second device or tablet, then point your phone camera at it.

3D Object
📷
Marker: hiro  ·  Shape: box
Requires: camera access and a browser that supports WebRTC (Chrome, Firefox, Safari 14+). Works best on mobile. HTTPS is required for camera — if on localhost, try serving over https or use a tunneling tool.
